# StartupBlink Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2019

## TL;DR
Canada ranks #3 globally in the 2019 rankings — a significant validation of ecosystem strength behind only the US and China. Year-over-year momentum tracking shows Canada's rising trajectory. Montreal ecosystem demonstrates upward momentum with early sector specialization.

## Key Data Points

1. **[entrepreneurship]** Canada ranks #3 globally, behind US and China — significant global position.
2. **[macro-trends]** Year-over-year momentum tracking shows Canada's rising trajectory.
3. **[entrepreneurship]** Canadian ecosystems moving from Globalization to Expansion phase in lifecycle model.
4. **[funding]** Cross-border capital flows between Canadian and US ecosystems increasing.
5. **[talent]** Growing diversity in founder backgrounds and experience across Canadian ecosystems.

## Quebec-Specific Findings

- Montreal recognized as a growing hub with distinct specializations
- Quebec policy environment supporting entrepreneurship noted
- Quebec ecosystems showing upward momentum in national rankings
- Toronto-Montreal dynamic: Toronto established as primary, Montreal positioned as growing secondary hub
- Early signs of specialized sectors developing in Quebec region

## Methodology & Limitations

- Builds on 2017 methodology with improved momentum calculations and expanded stakeholder network
- **Limitations:** Rapid ecosystem changes may outpace annual reporting; smaller ecosystems underrepresented

## Relevance to Our Work

Canada's #3 global ranking provides strong context for Quebec ecosystem positioning. Momentum tracking enables assessment of Quebec's growth relative to peers.
