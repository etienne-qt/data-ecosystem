# Dealroom: The Quebec Startup Ecosystem in Numbers (2024)

## TL;DR
Quebec's tech ecosystem is valued at ~$95B CAD with unprecedented growth momentum: 50% of ecosystem value came from startups founded since 2010 (vs. 36% national average), and Quebec ranks 4th globally in ecosystem growth since 2019. However, a critical paradox emerges: despite world-leading AI research, only 10.4% of Quebec VC went to AI companies (vs. 18.4% globally), while cleantech VC allocation (15%) significantly outpaces all benchmarks.

## Key Data Points
1. **Quebec ecosystem valuation:** ~$95B CAD
2. **Startup concentration:** 50% of ecosystem value from companies founded since 2010 (vs. 36% national)
3. **Global growth ranking:** 4th globally in ecosystem growth since 2019
4. **2023 funding:** Quebec startups raised $1.8B (down from $4.2B in 2022)
5. **Cleantech VC:** ~15% of Quebec VC since 2019 (outpacing Canada 12.3%, North America 10.2%, global 11.5%)
6. **AI VC allocation:** Only 10.4% went to AI companies (vs. 18.4% global average)

## Quebec-Specific Findings
- Quebec leads all benchmarks in cleantech VC allocation, reflecting hydroelectric heritage and established cleantech strengths
- Paradoxical AI funding gap: world-class research dominance (McGill, MILA, Vector) coexists with below-average AI startup VC allocation
- Report suggests AI capabilities may be embedded in companies classified under other verticals, indicating potential classification underestimation
- $4.2B→$1.8B funding decline (2022–2023) reflects broader market pullback affecting growth-stage deals
