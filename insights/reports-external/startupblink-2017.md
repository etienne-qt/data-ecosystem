# StartupBlink Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2017

## TL;DR
The inaugural StartupBlink report introduces Global Startup Ecosystem Rankings covering 1,000 cities across 100+ countries. Methodology combines Quantity Score (startup volume), Quality Score (unicorns, exits), and Business Environment Score (ease of doing business). Canada ranks as a major ecosystem globally, with Toronto and Vancouver as primary hubs and Montreal as a developing ecosystem.

## Key Data Points

1. **[entrepreneurship]** 1,000 cities analyzed across 100+ countries using three-pillar methodology.
2. **[macro-trends]** US dominates with multiple top-tier ecosystems; China, UK, Israel also strong.
3. **[entrepreneurship]** Canada established as top-10 global ecosystem nation.
4. **[macro-trends]** Ecosystem Lifecycle Model introduced: Activation, Globalization, Expansion, Integration phases.
5. **[incubation]** Assessment framework based on research with founders, investors, and ecosystem stakeholders.

## Quebec-Specific Findings

- Montreal identified as a developing startup ecosystem with growth potential
- Quebec City recognized as an emerging ecosystem in early activation phase
- Part of Canada's broader strong global positioning

## Methodology & Limitations

- Three-pillar assessment: Quantity, Quality, Business Environment
- Comprehensive global coverage combining quantitative data with qualitative stakeholder input
- **Limitations:** Point-in-time snapshots; momentum calculation methodology still early; cultural/regional variations may not be fully captured

## Relevance to Our Work

Establishes baseline framework for tracking Montreal/Quebec ecosystem position over time. Lifecycle Model useful for assessing Quebec's development stage.
