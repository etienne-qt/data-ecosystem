# Entrepreneurship — Research Insights

*Compiled from external reports. Updated each time a new report is ingested.*

## Key Themes

1. Inclusivity challenges in entrepreneurial pipeline — declining intentions among immigrants and women; female entrepreneurs show higher birth but lower survival rates
2. Scaleup gap as critical weakness — 22% of startups but only 14% scaleups from Quebec, constraining ecosystem maturity
3. Sector clusters driving company creation — AI, cleantech, and life sciences sectors driving 400+ cleantech companies, 753 life sciences companies, 53 Mila AI startups
