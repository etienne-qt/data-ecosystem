# Funding — Research Insights

*Compiled from external reports. Updated each time a new report is ingested.*

## Key Themes

1. Capital concentration and megadeal dominance — 26 megadeals consumed 66% of total 2025 funding
2. Quebec VC growth stagnation and 2025 downturn — flat 0.3% CAGR, now down 55% through Q3 2025
3. Foreign capital dependency and US investor retreat — 40% of Canadian VC from foreign sources; US participation dropped 32%
4. Exit drought constraining capital recycling — zero IPOs third consecutive year prevents exit-driven reinvestment
5. Strong early-stage but weak later-stage funding — Montreal early-stage at $914M (+78% above global avg), but scaleup gap evident
