# Canada — Research Insights

*Canada-wide findings from external reports (includes Quebec in aggregate).*

## Key Themes

1. **Chronic R&D underinvestment** — Canada at 1.81% of GDP (second-lowest G7), growth lagging OECD average; universities shoulder 35% of GERD vs. 16% OECD average
2. **Innovation Paradox** — Strong in top-cited papers and ease of entrepreneurship but second-to-last in VC among 20 peers; 15th of 20 with zero "A" grades on innovation report card
3. **Frozen exit market constraining ecosystem** — Zero VC-backed IPOs since 2022; median exit $30M vs. $232.7M in US; secondary transactions replacing exits as liquidity mechanism
4. **Foreign capital dependency** — US capital ~40% of total Canadian VC; US investor participation falling from 32% to 25% of deals amid trade tensions
5. **AI and sector concentration** — AI commands 30% of Canadian VC (record high); 65.6% of US VC to AI creating gravity pull
6. **Massive CVC participation gap** — Only 6% of Canadian public companies with $1B+ revenue participate in VC deals vs 40% in US; CVC 88% concentrated in finserv/media/tech
7. **Talent and commercialization bottlenecks at scale** — 40% of Fast 50 CEOs struggle with talent; 80% say commercialization is top challenge; Canadian unis earn 27% of US patent licensing revenue
8. **Declining global ecosystem position** — Canada slipped from #3 (StartupBlink 2019) to #5 (2025) with 5.3% decline; North America slowest regional growth at 15.7%
