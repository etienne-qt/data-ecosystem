## Key Themes

1. Post-pandemic recovery uneven across indicators — innovation declined 8pp, employment grew only 0.1%, one-third of businesses fragile
2. Capital concentration reshaping global VC — 26 megadeals took 66% of funding; US AI-driven concentration at 65.6% of VC value
3. Montreal cost advantage as competitive moat — 37% lower tech talent costs, $2.7B FDI supported in 2024, but ecosystem value fell 17%
