## Notes

- **Naming convention:** `{report_id}.pdf` — matches the `report_id` in `_registry.md` and the summary filename in `summaries/`
- **QT may already have access** to CVCA Intelligence, Réseau Capital data, and NACO reports through existing memberships
- **OECD reports** are often available through university library portals (HEC, McGill, UdeM)
- **PitchBook-NVCA** report is typically free on the NVCA website after providing an email address
- Once downloaded, check each file off in this manifest
