# Indice entrepreneurial québécois (IEQ) 2024

## TL;DR
The 15th annual IEQ survey of 21,450 Québécois (±1.2% margin of error) marks a post-crisis inflection point with mixed signals: innovation emerges as top strategic priority and mentoring adoption strengthens, but entrepreneurial intentions among immigrants and women are declining. Half of entrepreneurs perceive their business as robust; of the other half, one in three faces fragility and closure risk.

**Note:** Analysis based on press release and highlights; full report gated behind form submission.

## Key Data Points
1. **Survey Scale:** 21,450 Québécois surveyed (margin of error ±1.2%); 511 entrepreneurs in targeted sample (±4.3% margin)
2. **Edition:** 15th annual (2024)
3. **Mentoring Adoption:** 50.6% of entrepreneurs used mentoring as primary support mechanism
4. **Team Formation Preference:** Nearly 60% prefer starting a business in team/group setting
5. **Business Robustness Perception:** 50% perceive their business as robust; of the other half, one in three considers their enterprise fragile/at-risk
6. **Strategic Priorities:** Innovation is #1 priority for next three years
7. **Entrepreneurial Intentions:** Immigrants and women declining; men rebounding
8. **Business Restart Willingness:** ~75% of entrepreneurs who closed a business are willing to restart
9. **Support Mechanisms Ranking:** Mentoring, consulting, coaching (top three); incubators/accelerators rank fourth
10. **Organizational Forms:** New entrepreneurs favor "new organizational forms" (entreprise libérée, teal organizations)

## Quebec-Specific Findings
- 15th edition marks post-crisis inflection point with positive evolution of major indicators
- Persistent inclusivity challenges in entrepreneurial pipeline
- Mentoring, consulting, and coaching are the top three support mechanisms; incubators/accelerators rank fourth
- Approximately 75% of entrepreneurs who closed a business express willingness to restart, indicating resilience but also potential restart risk
- Innovation is the #1 strategic priority for the next three years across surveyed entrepreneurs

## Methodology & Limitations
- Two-part survey: (A) representative panel via Léger web survey (21,450 respondents); (B) targeted entrepreneur survey through 45 ecosystem partners (511 respondents)
- Margin of error: ±1.2% (Volet A), ±4.3% (Volet B)
- Covers full "chaîne entrepreneuriale" (entrepreneurial pipeline)
- Web panel may have self-selection bias; Volet B is non-probabilistic sampling
- Full report gated behind form submission; analysis based on press release and available highlights only
