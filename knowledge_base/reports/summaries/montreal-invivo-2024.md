# Montréal InVivo Rapport annuel 2024

## TL;DR
Quebec's life sciences and health technologies (LSHT) sector comprises 753 companies and 39,337 direct jobs, generating $6.5 billion in total GDP impact (1.6% of Quebec GDP). Greater Montreal accounts for ~80% of LSHT activity. In 2024, the sector attracted $422M in foreign direct investment. Analysis of top 20 Canadian life sciences exits shows 75%+ of investors are international, indicating potential leakage of economic returns to foreign capital.

## Key Data Points
1. **Sector Scale** – 753 companies; 39,337 direct jobs (biopharmaceuticals 21,493; medtech 15,643; natural health products 2,201)
2. **Economic Impact** – Direct GDP: $4.5B; Total (direct + indirect): $6.5B (~1.6% Quebec GDP)
3. **Geographic Concentration** – Greater Montreal: ~80% of Quebec LSHT activity
4. **FDI Attraction** – $422M CAD from 5 major life sciences projects in 2024
5. **Exit Capital Dynamics** – 75%+ of investors in Canada's top 20 life sciences exits are international
6. **Comparative Growth** – Canadian therapeutics VC grew from $122M (2013) to $842M (2024)

## Quebec-Specific Findings
- Life sciences is a major economic pillar with substantial employment base and GDP contribution
- Greater Montreal's concentration (80% of LSHT) makes the metropolitan region the undisputed Canadian life sciences hub
- FDI demonstrates sustained foreign interest; however, investor composition at exits indicates limited domestic institutional capital involvement
- Employment scale (39,337 direct jobs) indicates ecosystem sustainability and talent pipeline depth

## Methodology & Limitations
- Montréal InVivo data sourced from full PDF (released May 2025); adMare supplement data from summary only (white paper gated)
- Direct/indirect GDP split estimated; total figures represent combined economic contribution
- Exit investor data reflects top 20 transactions; broader market dynamics may differ
- FDI data reflects major project announcements in 2024; deployment timelines may extend beyond reporting year
