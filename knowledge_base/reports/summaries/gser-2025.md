# Startup Genome Global Startup Ecosystem Report (GSER) 2025

## TL;DR
Montreal ranked #39 globally (Top 40) with $27 billion in ecosystem value across H2 2022–2024, declining 17% versus the prior period. The ecosystem boasts 5 active unicorns and early-stage funding of $914M (78% above global average), but faces declining overall ecosystem value. Quebec City emerges as a North American talent and cost-efficiency contender.

## Key Data Points
1. **Montreal Global Ranking:** #39 (Top 40 status)
2. **Ecosystem Value:** $27 billion (H2 2022–2024), declining 17% vs. prior period (vs. 14% global average decline)
3. **Active Unicorns:** 5
4. **Early-Stage Funding (Seed + Series A):** $914M (78% above global average of $514.8M)
5. **Median Seed Round:** $1.5M (vs. $889K global avg.)
6. **Median Series A:** $10.9M (vs. $6.8M global avg.)
7. **Total VC Funding (2020–2024):** $9B
8. **Exit Amount (2020–2024):** $7.2B
9. **AI Sector:** AI commands 40% of all global VC
10. **Montreal Tech Salaries:** Software engineers earn $68K (vs. $52K global avg.)
11. **Quebec City Rankings:** #3 North America for "Bang for Buck"; #4 for Affordable Talent
12. **Espace CDPQ:** Houses 26 VC funds (Canada's largest venture capital community)
13. **Centech:** Ranked among world's top 10 university incubators

## Quebec-Specific Findings
- Montreal positioned as "an AI powerhouse" with AI, Big Data & Analytics as its top sub-sector
- Quebec City ranked #3 in North America for "Bang for Buck" (cost-efficiency) and #4 for Affordable Talent
- Espace CDPQ houses 26 VC funds—Canada's largest venture capital community
- Centech ranked among the world's top 10 university incubators
- Early-stage capital concentration in Montreal far exceeds global benchmarks
