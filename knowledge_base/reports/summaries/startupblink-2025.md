# StartupBlink Global Startup Ecosystem Index 2025

## TL;DR
The 2025 Index ranks 1,473 cities and 118 countries on startup ecosystem health. Global ecosystem growth stands at 20.9%, with AI, cleantech, foodtech, and hardware driving innovation. Canada ranks 5th globally but experienced a 5.3% decline from previous year, signaling competitive pressures from Singapore and Israel.

## Key Data Points

1. **[entrepreneurship]** 1,473 cities and 118 countries ranked using 100+ metrics across Quantity, Quality, and Business Environment subscores.
2. **[macro-trends]** Global ecosystem growth rate: 20.9% (2024-2025).
3. **[macro-trends]** Regional growth leaders: Asia-Pacific (27.4%), Europe (26.2%), Middle East & Africa (24.9%); North America slowest at 15.7%.
4. **[macro-trends]** Fastest-growing industries: Foodtech (46.1%), Hardware & IoT (45.4%), Energy & Environment (40.2%).
5. **[entrepreneurship]** Top countries stable: US, UK, Israel, Singapore, Canada (positions 1-5).
6. **[entrepreneurship]** Canada ranked 5th globally, experiencing 5.3% decline from previous year.
7. **[macro-trends]** EdTech only sector with negative growth (-2.3%).
8. **[macro-trends]** Top cities: San Francisco, New York, London, Los Angeles, Beijing.

- New Annual Ecosystem Growth indicator comparing 2024-2025
- Proprietary crowdsourced database with 200,000+ entities
- **Limitations:** Does not capture qualitative ecosystem maturity factors
