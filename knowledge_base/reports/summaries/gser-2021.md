# Global Startup Ecosystem Report 2021

## TL;DR
2021 marked remarkable growth for global startup ecosystems post-pandemic. Venture funding soared to $288 billion in H1 2021 (95% increase from H1 2020). 91 ecosystems created at least one unicorn. The report highlights democratization across continents and the emergence of Silicon Valley talent out-migration as a structural shift.

## Key Data Points

1. **[funding]** H1 2021 VC funding reached $288 billion, up from $148 billion in H1 2020 (95% increase).
2. **[exits]** Exits showing 20% year-over-year growth in dollar value.
3. **[macro-trends]** 91 ecosystems created at least one unicorn in 2020; new unicorns emerging in unexpected places (Montevideo, Dallas, Busan, Pune).
4. **[talent]** Talent out-migration from Silicon Valley notable; driven by cost constraints and remote work adoption.
5. **[funding]** Women-led company funding declined 27% in 2020 vs 2019; Black founders quadrupled funding in H1 2021.
6. **[macro-trends]** Top 100 ecosystems added $193 billion in ecosystem value.
7. **[cleantech-sector]** Entrepreneurship for Climate (E4C) initiative launched to scale cleantech solutions.
